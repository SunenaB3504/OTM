Times Tables

Learn and Practice Multiplication Tables

1. Understanding Times Tables

Times tables help us quickly find the result of multiplying two numbers. They are essential for solving math problems faster.

Example

To find 3 × 4, we can use the times table to see that 3 × 4 = 12.

2. Practice with Flash Cards

3. Real-Life Examples

Example 1: Buying Apples

If each bag has 5 apples and you buy 4 bags, how many apples do you have in total?

4 × 5 = 20 apples

Example 2: Counting Wheels

If each car has 4 wheels and there are 6 cars, how many wheels are there in total?

6 × 4 = 24 wheels

4. Quizzes and Games

For each multiplication fact, write the corresponding division facts:
