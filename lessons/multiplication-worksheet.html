Answer Key

Draw and Count:

  • 2 × 3 = 6
  • 3 × 2 = 6
  • 4 × 2 = 8

Write the Repeated Addition:

  • 3 × 4 = 12 (Addition: 4 + 4 + 4 = 12)
  • 5 × 3 = 15 (Addition: 3 + 3 + 3 + 3 + 3 = 15)
  • 2 × 6 = 12 (Addition: 6 + 6 = 12)

Count the Groups:

  • 3 × 3 = 9
  • 2 × 4 = 8

Real-World Problems:

  • 1. 3 × 5 = 15 toys
  • 2. 4 × 2 = 8 wheels
  • 3. 5 × 3 = 15 apples
