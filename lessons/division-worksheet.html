Answer Key

Convert Division to Multiplication:

  • 14 ÷ 2 = 7: 2 × 7 = 14
  • 18 ÷ 3 = 6: 3 × 6 = 18
  • 24 ÷ 8 = 3: 8 × 3 = 24

Convert Multiplication to Division:

  • 5 × 6 = 30: 30 ÷ 5 = 6 and 30 ÷ 6 = 5
  • 9 × 4 = 36: 36 ÷ 9 = 4 and 36 ÷ 4 = 9

Complete the Fact Families:

  • Fact Family with 4, 7, and 28:
    • 4 × 7 = 28
    • 7 × 4 = 28
    • 28 ÷ 4 = 7
    • 28 ÷ 7 = 4
  • Fact Family with 6, 5, and 30:
    • 6 × 5 = 30
    • 5 × 6 = 30
    • 30 ÷ 6 = 5
    • 30 ÷ 5 = 6

Division with Objects:

  • 15 objects divided into 3 equal groups:
    • Division fact: 15 ÷ 3 = 5
    • Multiplication fact: 3 × 5 = 15
  • 12 objects divided into 4 equal groups:
    • Division fact: 12 ÷ 4 = 3
    • Multiplication fact: 4 × 3 = 12

Real-World Problems:

  • Problem 1: 24 ÷ 6 = 4 stickers per friend; 6 × 4 = 24
  • Problem 2: 42 ÷ 7 = 6 boxes; 7 × 6 = 42
  • Problem 3: 35 ÷ 5 = 7 cars; 5 × 7 = 35

Check Your Multiplication:

  • 5 × 7 = 35: 35 ÷ 5 = 7, 35 ÷ 7 = 5 (Yes, it's correct)
  • 6 × 6 = 36: 36 ÷ 6 = 6 (Yes, it's correct)
  • 4 × 8 = 30: 30 ÷ 4 = 7.5, 30 ÷ 8 = 3.75 (No, it's not correct. 4 × 8 = 32)
